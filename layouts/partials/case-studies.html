Financing-as-a-Service Platform at topi

Context

topi enables retailers to offer flexible B2B financing at point-of-sale. At Series A, the product needed to scale fast while supporting merchant-specific business logic.

Challenge

Long onboarding cycles, hardcoded logic, poor configurability for new partners like MediaMarkt and Deutsche Telekom.

Actions

Led modular redesign of the platform. Introduced dynamic configuration engine, real-time observability, and an internal tooling layer.

Results

Reduced onboarding from 6 weeks to 1 week. Scaled to hundreds of enterprise merchants with zero downtime.
